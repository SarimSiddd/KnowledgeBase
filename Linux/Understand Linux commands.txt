Understand Linux commands


ls [arg] ...

- arg is an optional argument
- ... means we can supply multiples of argument arg

ls [arg1 | arg2 | arg3]

- | means we can only supply one of the three arguments

ls --all

- "--" means to interpret the command as a whole word

ls -all

- "-all" means the arguments are interepreted seperately and not as a whole word

manpages

- Can access manual pages by typing man
